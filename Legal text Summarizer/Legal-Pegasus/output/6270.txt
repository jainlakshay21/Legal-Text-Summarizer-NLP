The Government of India issued a scheme for the benefit of ex military officials. The State of Uttar Pradesh also adopted the same scheme. Under that scheme the ex military officials were appointed on Contract basis for a fixed term which could be extended from time to time subject to the suitability of the official concerned but not beyond 58 years of age.
